Podology Clinic

Your podiatrist will look at your feet and check for any signs of issues like fungus, warts, corns, bunions, and other similar foot problems. The doctor will look at your toenails for any signs of discoloration or brittleness. During your appointment, the podiatrist will also check your lower legs to assess for signs of poor circulation. You may be asked to walk for the doctor so that they can evaluate you for any gait problems.
